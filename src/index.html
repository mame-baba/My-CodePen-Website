Suits and Tie Specs

  1. Our suits are made of an open woven, high quality polyester. This allows the suit to breathe and it gives the fabric a natural feel, just like a normal (boring) suit would.

  2. Our suits are equipped with inner lining, inside pockets and pockets on the front. Our pants have pockets both in the front and in the back.

  3. The pockets in the back even have buttons so you can close them and not have to worry about losing your wallet while having the time of your life.
